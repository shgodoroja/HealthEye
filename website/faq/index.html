FAQ

Frequently asked questions about HealthEye

Answers for coaches evaluating HealthEye for weekly Apple Health check-ins, reporting, privacy, and pricing.

Product & workflow

Does HealthEye work without internet access?+
Yes. Core workflows — import, analysis, and report generation — run locally on your Mac. An internet connection is only needed for billing and optional updates.
Does HealthEye support Apple Watch data?+
Yes. Apple Watch metrics written to Apple Health (sleep, HRV, resting heart rate, workouts, steps) are included when present in the Apple Health export.
How does the attention score work?+
The attention score combines a recovery composite (45%), workout load (25%), steps (15%), and a completeness penalty (15%). Each score shows metric-level drivers and rule explanations so every change is traceable.
How many clients does HealthEye support?+
HealthEye is designed for independent coaches managing 20 to 100 clients. The triage dashboard keeps the weekly review fast at that scale.
Do clients need to install anything?+
No. Clients only export their Apple Health data from iPhone and send you the file. HealthEye runs on the coach's Mac or iPad.

Privacy & data

How are client records stored?+
HealthEye uses a local SQLite database encrypted at rest. Client imports stay on your Mac unless you export or share them yourself.
Is HealthEye a medical device?+
No. HealthEye produces wellness and coaching insights for professional coaches. It is not intended for diagnosis or treatment.
Can I export or delete client data?+
Yes. HealthEye supports machine-readable export of client records and reports, plus archive and delete operations. You own your data.

Pricing & platforms

Can I try HealthEye before subscribing?+
Yes. HealthEye includes a 14-day trial with full coach workflow access. No credit card is required to start.
Does HealthEye work on iPad?+
Yes. HealthEye is a universal app for macOS and iPadOS, with the triage dashboard optimized for both form factors.
What are the subscription plans?+
Solo Coach at $39/month for up to 30 active clients, and Pro Coach at $79/month for up to 120 active clients. See the pricing page for the latest details.
