Privacy & Data Controls

Privacy-first, local analysis by design

HealthEye is built around a simple principle: coaches should be able to analyze Apple Health exports on Mac and generate reports without losing control of client data.

Data handling principles

  • Local-first analysis workflow on Mac
  • No diagnosis or medical treatment claims
  • Data completeness and score explanations shown in-app
  • Export and deletion controls available to the user

Data ownership controls

  • Export machine-readable data for portability
  • Archive clients when no longer active
  • Delete client records and generated reports when needed
  • Review generated output before sharing
